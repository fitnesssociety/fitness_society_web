Privacy Policy for fitnesSociety

Last updated: May 1, 2023


Introduction

fitnesSociety ("we," "us," "our," or "the App") values the privacy of our users ("you" or "the user"). This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and disclose personal information from users of our mobile fitness application (the "App"). By using the App, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this policy.


Information We Collect

We may collect personal information from you when you register for an account on fitnesSociety, create a personalized meal and workout plan, use the buddy feature, or interact with the social networking aspect of the App. The personal information we may collect includes, but is not limited to:


    • Your name and email address

    • Your height, weight, and fitness goals

    • Your progress tracking data

    • Your interactions with other users in fitnesSociety

We may also collect non-personal information such as your device information, app usage data, and IP address.


How We Use Your Information

We may use your personal information to:

    • Provide you with personalized meal and workout plans

    • Track your progress towards your fitness goals

    • Connect you with a buddy or fitness coach

    • Allow you to connect with other users and share your workout plans

    • Improve fitnesSociety and user experience

    • Send you promotional or informational emails


How We Share Your Information

We may share your personal information with third-party service providers who help us operate fitnesSociety and provide you with the features and services you use. We may also share your personal information with law enforcement or other third parties when required by law or to protect our rights or the rights of others.


Your Choices

You can choose to limit the personal information we collect from you by not providing us with certain information or by deleting your account. You can also choose to opt-out of receiving promotional emails from us by following the unsubscribe link at the bottom of the email.


Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the personal information we collect from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no security measures can provide absolute protection, and we cannot guarantee the security of your personal information.


Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, and the updated version will be posted on fitnesSociety. Your continued use of the App after the changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.


Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at joinfitnesssociety@gmail.com

By using fitnesSociety, you consent to this Privacy Policy and our data practices as described herein.
