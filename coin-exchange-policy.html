fitnesSociety Coin Exchange Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2024


1. Introduction

Thank you for participating in our coin reward program. This policy outlines the terms and conditions for redeeming rewards using the coins earned within our app. By engaging in our coin reward program, you agree to adhere to the following guidelines.


2. Availability of Items

The availability of items for redemption is subject to change without prior notice.

We strive to maintain a diverse selection of rewards; however, we cannot guarantee the continuous availability of specific items.


3. Approval Process

All reward redemptions are subject to approval by the administrators or moderators of the app.

We reserve the right to decline any redemption request at our discretion.


4. Deduction of Coins

Once a redemption request is approved, the corresponding number of coins will be deducted from the user's account.

This action is irreversible, and users cannot undo the deduction of coins.


5. Rejection of Orders

Redemption orders may be rejected due to constraints such as item and size availability.

In such cases, the coins deducted for the redemption will be refunded to the user's account.


6. Misuse of the Redeem Rewards Feature

Any misuse or abuse of the redeem rewards feature may result in suspension or banning of the user's account.

Misuse includes, but is not limited to, attempting to exploit loopholes in the redemption process or engaging in fraudulent activity.


7. Non-Transferability of Coins

Coins earned within the app are non-transferable and cannot be sent to another user's account for redemption purposes.


8. Leaderboard Rewards

Leaderboard rewards are time-limited.

If a reward is not redeemed during the allowed period, it will expire, and users will no longer be able to redeem it.


General Guidelines

Users are responsible for ensuring the accuracy of their redemption requests, including selecting the correct item and size (if applicable).

We reserve the right to modify or terminate the coin exchange program at any time without prior notice.

Users are encouraged to review the available rewards and their corresponding coin values regularly.


By participating in our coin exchange program, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to abide by the terms outlined in this policy.

We appreciate your cooperation and look forward to providing you with a rewarding experience within our app.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our coin exchange policy, please contact our support team for assistance.
